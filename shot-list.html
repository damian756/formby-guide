Tips for Today
  • Beach: Before 10am or after 3pm — better light, fewer people.
  • Pinewoods: Morning light through the pines is the money shot — the path from the NT car park toward the beach.
  • Squirrels: Best around the feeders near the main trail — early morning. Don't bring dogs.
  • OG crops: Shoot landscape (3:2 or wider). You can crop to 1200×630 in editing — don't try to frame exactly in camera.
  • Golden hour on the beach will look far better than midday flat light for the hero shots.
